By Jean-Marc Dupont · Published on 07/06/2026

After five consecutive record years driven by Chinese demand, Bordeaux wine merchants are facing a sharp downturn in exports to Asia. But far from panicking, the most agile players are already pivoting toward new markets — the United States, the United Kingdom post-Brexit, and emerging demand in Southeast Asia.

The numbers are stark: Bordeaux exports to China fell by 34% in volume in 2025, according to the CIVB (Bordeaux Wine Trade Council). For négociants who had built significant exposure to Chinese buyers during the boom years, the adjustment has been painful.
