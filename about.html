About sudouest.com

sudouest.com is the international edition of Sud Ouest, France's leading regional daily newspaper, published since 1944 and headquartered in Bordeaux.

This site offers a curated selection of articles from sudouest.fr, automatically translated into English, Spanish and German using artificial intelligence, targeting international readers — tourists, expats, and anyone interested in the South West of France.

Editorial disclaimer

All articles published on sudouest.com are originally written in French by our editorial team and translated automatically. While we strive for accuracy, machine translation may contain errors. The original French-language version remains the authoritative source and is available on sudouest.fr.
